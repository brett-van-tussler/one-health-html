Proportion of emm49 Genomes by Month

The above graph displays the proportion of isolates characterized as emm49 by month compared to the overall number of isolates sequenced.
Approximately 30% of the isolates received for genomic characterization have been identified as emm49. Several ongoing genomic clusters within this emm-type (samples collected within a designated timeframe separated by 0-2 mutations) have also been discovered. These clusters, many of which are connected with healthcare settings, may not have otherwise been detected through routine public health surveillance mechanisms.
