Total One Health Samples: 775


In 2021, TGen launched a multifaceted surveillance project to conduct surveillance for SARS-CoV-2 in companion animals, working dogs, animals in human care at zoological settings, and free-ranging wildlife. We have conducted numerous One Health investigations to understand the impacts of SARS-CoV-2 in animals, as well as characterized viral variants of infected dogs and cats through genomic sequencing.
